Case Study

Amantronic Defect Detection

A dual-purpose system for RapidKL trains that combines audio-based mechanical defect detection with real-time rolling stock tracking.

Key Tech

Audio Frequency Analysis

Tech Stack

  • Laravel
  • IoT / GPS
  • MQTT
  • Web Dashboard

Context

VISI Innoworks

Client: RapidKL / NI Partner

01. The Problem

The operator faced two distinct challenges: identifying mechanical defects in trains before catastrophic failure, and monitoring rolling stock location in real-time, especially in underground tunnels where GPS signals are lost.

Additionally, safety was a concern for trackside workers, requiring a system to warn them of approaching trains to prevent accidents.

02. The Solution

We developed Amantronic, a unified IoT solution that serves two critical functions:

1. Audio Defect Detection: The system analyzes sound data from the moving train, converting it into frequency signals to identify specific mechanical defects automatically.

2. Rolling Stock Tracking: An on-board GPS tracker transmits location data via 4G/5G every 0.5 seconds. The system synthesizes this data to calculate ground speed and predicted trajectory, ensuring continuous tracking even in underground tunnels.

03. My Contribution

  • Leadership: Served as a key developer contributing to the system design and technical implementation, collaborating closely with stakeholders to translate operational requirements into a reliable, scalable monitoring platform.
  • Development: Designed and developed a web-based monitoring system that provides centralized visibility into train condition data, featuring responsive dashboards and real-time status updates.
  • Integration: Implemented functionality to capture and process sound data, converting raw audio signals into analyzable frequency data to support early detection of mechanical defects in train components.
  • Optimization: Integrated Google Maps API to deliver a real-time train tracking feature, visualizing live train locations and movement data to enhance situational awareness and maintenance decision-making.

04. Key Features

  • Frequency Signal Analysis: Converts raw audio data into frequency spectrums to detect anomalies indicative of mechanical failure.
  • Trajectory & Geo-Fencing: The Cloud system calculates the future path of rolling stocks to perform geo-fencing analysis.
  • Underground Tracking: Capable of deriving geo-locations and ground speed when the train is operating underground without GPS.
